OneApply

One stop solution for your high school applications

OneApply eliminates the hassle associated with High School applications in
New York by providing a secure, convenient, and customized portal for your application needs.

You can be a student and get access to all the High Schools across New York
or you can and be member of the admission committee for your High School
and get access to all the applications submitted for your school.

Features

Whether you're a student, an admission staff member, or a student's mentor, OneApply has something in store for you.

As Student

You will be able to select, apply, and get notified about your high school applications using OneApply.

Create an account to gain access to

400+ high schools and programs across New York
Customized results using tools like search, favorites, filters
1 application system for all your selected schools
List of all your applications, distributed over programs, in one place
Request and manage your mentor's recommendation

As Admission Staff

You'll be able to list, filter, and get detailed information of all submitted applications to your high school using OneApply.

Create an account to gain access to

All submitted applications in one place
Detailed information for any student's application
Accept/Reject, subsequently notifying, any student's application
Customized results using filters over programs, gpa, and more

As Mentor

You will be able to access recommendation requests and submit detailed recommendations securely.

OneApply provides you

Access to student's requested recommendation without creating an account
Secure submission of the requested recommendations
